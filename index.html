I utilized the powerful Transformer(RoBERTa) model for sentiment analysis, which allowed me to gain a better understanding of the emotions and attitudes expressed in text. Whether it was analyzing product reviews, social media posts, or customer feedback, this model enabled me to explore the underlying sentiments conveyed by individuals. These valuable insights proved instrumental in driving decision-making, informing effective marketing strategies, and facilitating product and service improvements based on customer feedback.
